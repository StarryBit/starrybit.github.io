Designed for Fast Iteration

StarryDrive is a production-ready vehicle system for Roblox Studio that makes building drivable vehicles simple, predictable, and fast. Transform almost any vehicle model into a fully functional car with an intuitive setup panel, no hours of tweaking constraints or rebuilding suspension systems required.

Choose from built-in presets like Car and Kart, or fine-tune every aspect of the driving experience yourself, including suspension, steering, acceleration, top speed, drift strength, lift, and more. Every configuration is stored as Roblox Attributes, making vehicles easy to revisit, rebalance, and iterate on throughout development.

Whether you're building an arcade racer, roleplay experience, or open-world driving game, StarryDrive provides a reliable foundation that lets you spend more time building your game, not fighting physics.

Two Modes, One System

Every Roblox game has different driving requirements. StarryDrive includes two physics modes designed around different types of experiences, giving developers control over how their vehicles behave.

Block Mode

Built for traditional Roblox Part-based maps. Provides stable, predictable arcade handling with responsive steering and smooth suspension.

Terrain Mode

Designed for open worlds and uneven landscapes. Vehicles adapt dynamically to terrain slopes, bumps, and changing surfaces.

Why I Built StarryDrive

Like many Roblox developers, I spent countless hours trying to build vehicles using Roblox's constraint system. While constraints are incredibly powerful, creating a vehicle that feels stable, responsive, and reliable often requires constant tweaking—and sometimes one unexpected bump is enough to send everything flying.

I built StarryDrive to remove that frustration. Instead of repeatedly balancing dozens of physics values, developers can configure a vehicle in minutes and focus on creating great gameplay instead of debugging suspension.
