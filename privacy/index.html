Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important.

starrybitdev.com collects limited information to improve the site and provide support for purchases.

  • Basic site visit information through analytics (when enabled)
  • Product purchase information handled through itch.io
  • Your Discord username if you provide it for purchase verification or support

Payments and purchases are handled by itch.io. starrybitdev.com does not process or store payment information.

Any information collected is only used to improve the site, understand product performance, and provide customer support.

If you have questions about this policy or your information, you can contact me through the Discord server.
